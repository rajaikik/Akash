Q.1 In this question, three statements are given, followed by two conclusions numbered I and II. Assuming the statements to be true, even if they seem to be at variance with commonly known facts, decide which of the conclusions logically follows/follow from the statements.

Statements:
I. Some rings are fingers.
II. Some fingers are toes.
III. No toe is an earring.

Conclusions:
I. No earring is a ring.
II. Some toes are rings.
Ans 1. Neither conclusion I nor II follows
2. Both conclusions I and II follow
3. Only conclusion I follows
4. Only conclusion II follows
